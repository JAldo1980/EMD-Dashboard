The Alternative You Haven't Considered

Eliminating Thermal Stress
and Costly Tooling.

Why more aerospace, medical, and electronics engineers are switching from stamping and laser cutting to photochemical etching.

Zero Tooling Wear

Digital tooling means design modifications cost nothing and lead times don't stretch. Iterate on your geometry freely — from prototype to production, your first quote is your final quote.

100% Burr-Free

Chemical removal ensures pristine edge quality without the mechanical stress or deformations caused by stamping or laser cutting. Every edge is dimensionally perfect under microscope inspection.

Intricate Geometry

Complexity doesn't increase cost. Etch ultra-fine meshes, variable depths, and complex profiles simultaneously on a single sheet — what takes multiple stamping operations costs the same as a simple part.
