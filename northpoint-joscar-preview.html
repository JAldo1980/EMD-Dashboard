What is JOSCAR?

JOSCAR — the Joint Supply Chain Accreditation Register — is the pre-qualification standard for the UK's defence, aerospace, and security supply chain. Managed by Hellios on behalf of prime contractors including BAE Systems, Babcock, Leonardo, and Rolls-Royce, JOSCAR enables supply chain managers to assess, verify, and onboard suppliers without repeating due diligence from scratch on every new programme.

Northpoint has achieved Stage 2 JOSCAR accreditation — the full assessment tier. Stage 2 goes beyond basic registration. It involves a comprehensive review across six core areas of business conduct, and is subject to annual renewal — so our status on the register is always current, not a historic snapshot.

Our registration is publicly verifiable at any time through the JOSCAR portal at hellios.com. No additional pre-qualification from your team is required.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is JOSCAR accreditation?

JOSCAR (Joint Supply Chain Accreditation Register) is the pre-qualification standard used by the UK defence, aerospace, and security industry. Managed by Hellios on behalf of prime contractors including BAE Systems, Babcock, Leonardo, and Rolls-Royce, it provides a single shared register of pre-qualified suppliers — reducing duplication and accelerating onboarding across the sector.

What is the difference between JOSCAR Stage 1 and Stage 2?

Stage 1 is a basic registration level covering introductory compliance information. Stage 2 is the full assessment tier, involving a detailed review of health and safety, quality assurance, anti-bribery, information security, environmental management, and financial standing. Most prime contractor supply chains require Stage 2. Northpoint holds Stage 2 accreditation.

Which prime contractors use the JOSCAR register?

JOSCAR is used by the majority of the UK's defence and aerospace prime contractors, including BAE Systems, Babcock International, Leonardo UK, Rolls-Royce, QinetiQ, Thales UK, MBDA, and Serco. If your programme involves any of these organisations as prime or Tier 1 contractors, JOSCAR Stage 2 registration is typically a supply chain requirement.

How do I verify Northpoint's JOSCAR status?

Our Stage 2 accreditation is publicly verifiable at any time through the JOSCAR portal at hellios.com. Search for Northpoint Ltd. No prior contact with us is required to confirm our status. Alternatively, contact our compliance team directly for a copy of our current registration certificate.

Does Northpoint work on programmes outside the defence sector?

Yes. While our JOSCAR accreditation is specific to the defence, aerospace, and security supply chain, Northpoint works across multiple regulated sectors. We are one of only three UK companies approved to apply DWI-compliant coatings for drinking water infrastructure, and we work with contractors on the UK's AMP8 water investment cycle. Our compliance infrastructure applies across all the sectors we serve.

Does Northpoint need to be re-assessed each year?

Yes. JOSCAR Stage 2 is subject to annual renewal by Hellios. Our accreditation is reviewed and renewed against the current standard each year — so our status on the register always reflects our current compliance position, not a historic snapshot from initial registration.
